## Should you search only Elkhorn Springs?

If lifestyle amenities and village character are priorities, anchoring on Elkhorn Springs makes sense. If budget or floor plan drives the decision, compare adjacent Skye Canyon neighborhoods with similar schools and commute times.

## How Dr. Jan Duffy structures the search

We start with non-negotiables—beds, garage count, single-story preference, HOA appetite—then layer village personality and resale risk. That keeps you from chasing listings that look good online but fail on-site.

## Want help interpreting new construction incentives?

Builder concessions change monthly. Ask for a side-by-side of resale vs new for your timeline before you put down earnest money.
