## What makes Elkhorn Springs different?

Elkhorn Springs is a master-planned pocket inside Skye Canyon. Inventory spans gated villages, resale neighborhoods, and builder phases—so two homes with the same bed count can perform very differently at resale.

## HOA and insurance

Request the latest HOA budget, reserves study, and rules. Confirm what exterior maintenance covers, whether short-term rentals are restricted, and whether your lender has approved the project for your loan type.

## Tour like a local

Drive your evening commute, listen for arterial noise on patios, and confirm how afternoon sun hits the backyard. If schools matter, verify boundaries with CCSD—do not rely on marketing maps alone.
